## What this project contains

Only **original companion material**: our own outlines, summaries, glossary,
thematic index, structural analysis, and tooling. Wherever the reader needs the
actual text, we **link out** to the official source,
[erisale.com](https://www.erisale.com/?locale=en).

Our companion notes are **never** presented as Nursî's words, and this project is
**not** a simplified or modernised version of the Risale ("sadeleştirme"). It is
a study aid that points to the original.
