The Fifteenth Flash is not a treatise but a short notice. It consists of the
**indexes** to *Sözler* (The Words), *Mektûbat* (the Letters), and *Lem'alar*
(The Flashes, the First through the Fourteenth) — reference apparatus that has
been kept in those volumes rather than printed again here.

This companion entry simply records that, so the numbering reads clearly; there
is no separate treatise to summarise.
