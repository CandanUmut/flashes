As with the Eighth Flash, the text here gives only a short notice: the Eighteenth
Flash has been published in **Sikke-i Tasdîk-i Gaybî** (*The Ratifying Stamp of
the Unseen*) and in hand-duplicated copies of the collection, rather than printed
in full at this place.

Because our source does not carry the treatise itself here, this companion entry
simply records where it lives, with no summary invented from absent text. If the
Sikke-i Tasdîk-i Gaybî version is later brought into scope, a full companion note
can be written from it.
