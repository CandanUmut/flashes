The Twenty-Seventh Flash is a short notice rather than a treatise. It consists of
the **defence speeches Nursî gave at the Eskişehir trial**, which were published
in hand-duplicated editions of the collection and, in part, in his biography,
rather than printed in full at this place.

Because our source does not carry the speeches themselves here, this companion
entry records only where they live, with no summary invented from absent text.
