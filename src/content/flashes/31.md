The Thirty-First Flash is a short notice. Its material was **assigned to The Rays
Collection** (*Şuâlar*): the Fourteenth Ray became the defence speeches and
letters of the Afyon court, and the Fifteenth Ray was published under the name
*El-Hüccetü'z-Zehrâ* (The Shining Proof).

This companion entry records only where that material lives; there is no separate
treatise to summarise at this place.
