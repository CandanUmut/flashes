The Thirty-Second Flash is a short notice. It is **Lemeât**, described as the
last work of the "Old Said" — a piece that took on a poetic form and was composed
in twenty days during the month of Ramadan — and it has been included in *Sözler*
(The Words) rather than reproduced here.

This companion entry records only where the work lives; there is no separate
treatise to summarise at this place.
