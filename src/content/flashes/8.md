At this point in The Flashes the text gives only a short notice: the Eighth
Flash has been published in printed editions of **Sikke-i Tasdîk-i Gaybî** (*The
Ratifying Stamp of the Unseen*) and in hand-duplicated copies of the collection,
rather than set out in full here.

Because our source does not carry the treatise itself at this place, this
companion entry simply records where it lives, with no summary invented from
absent text. If the Sikke-i Tasdîk-i Gaybî version is later brought into scope,
a full companion note can be written from it.
